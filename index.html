Software

Cub & CubOS

The two layers that turn a CNC gantry into a self-driving lab.

CubOS

Instrument operating system

Firmware and motion-control layer that runs on the gantry. Handles G-code generation, instrument head coordination, and real-time safety checks so hardware behaves predictably across every well.

Cub

Experiment orchestration

Python-based application that designs experiments, sends jobs to CubOS, collects structured data, and feeds results into Bayesian optimization for closed-loop autonomous discovery.
